AntiDragonEggStack 2.1.2

Remove Stacks of Dragon Eggs

  • Overview
  • Updates (30)
  • Reviews (98)
  • Version History
  • Discussion
  1. Contributors:
    moomaxie

  2. Encountered a case of excess dragon eggs or suspected cheating?
  3. Dragon Egg Control is the solution you need.
  4. This plugin ensures fair play by automatically managing dragon egg quantities.
  5. Any surplus eggs are swiftly removed, leaving only one pristine egg in a player's inventory.
  6. No need for complex configurations—keep your server balanced effortlessly!
